Privacy & what we store

Pin the Planet is built around being lightweight - no accounts, no email, no tracking across sites. The only things stored are on your own device.

In your browser's local storage, we save:

  • Your name and game preferences (rounds, timer, tone, etc) so they persist between visits
  • Your daily challenge streak, best scores per date, and solo bests
  • Your current room session so a refresh doesn't kick you out mid-game
  • A small queue of recent in-app events for debugging

We also use PostHog for anonymous usage analytics (which features people actually use, where things break) so the game keeps improving. No personal data is sold or shared.

Clear your browser's site data for pintheplanet.co.uk to delete everything we've stored locally.
